Frequently asked questions

Do I need coding skills?

No. The WebsiteToApp template lets you convert a website without writing code. Optional JavaScript customization is available if you want more control over the mobile experience.

Does it work with Google Sites?

Yes! Google Sites is one of the most popular use cases. Paste your Google Sites URL into the template, customize, and export your app. See our video tutorial for a step-by-step walkthrough.

Can I publish to Google Play and the App Store?

Yes. Appery.io lets you export store-ready APK (Android) and IPA (iOS) binaries. You'll need developer accounts with Google and Apple to publish, but the app packaging is handled by the platform.

Is this just a WebView wrapper?

The WebsiteToApp template loads your site in a native app shell — but Appery.io gives you full control to customize branding, hide elements, add splash screens, and export real installable binaries. It's the fastest path from web to mobile, not a shortcut that limits you.

How long does it take?

Most users create a working prototype in under 30 minutes. A polished, store-ready app can be done in a few hours depending on customization needs.

What if I need help?

Appery.io offers documentation, a community forum, and Support Pack service plans with professional development assistance. Contact us to discuss your project.
